Welcome to Trending Trails

About

Trending Trails is at its core designed to help its users live a more active and healthier lifestyle. To accomplish this goal, Trending Trails helps its users to find enjoyable outdoor activities. These outdoor activities may include but are not limited to hiking and biking trails, mountain climbing and fishing.

In the past 10 years, there has been an increase in participants of outdoor activities. However, we believe that one of the biggest things gatekeeping more people from getting involved is either not knowing where to find good hiking trails or not having people who share their enthusiasm in the outdoors. Trending Trails aims to solve both problems simultaneously. By crowd sourcing a database of all sorts of outdoor activities, our users will be sure to find activities near them, while also being able to form groups with other users who are interested in exploring the same things.

With the ability for users to list new activities, other users to verify and rate these activities and for users to collaborate to explore these activities together, the possibilities are endless.
